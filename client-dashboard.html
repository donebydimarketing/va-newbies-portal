Best practices
The one task rule
Give your VA one clear task at a time. Clarity produces better results than a long to-do list.
The 21-day method
Trust the process. It takes 21 days to build a reliable working rhythm with your VA.
Feedback before niche
Give feedback early and often. A VA who knows your standards becomes irreplaceable.
Communication standards
Set response time expectations on day one. Ambiguity slows everything down.
